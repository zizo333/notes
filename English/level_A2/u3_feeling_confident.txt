# Feeling and Moods

    * calm - relaxed - happy - confident - proud - exicited - surprised - pleased(مسرور/راضى)
    * cheerful(مبتهج) - amused(متسلى) - irritated(مغتاظ) - angry - annoyed(متضايق)
    * furious(مستشيط غضبا) - sad - unhappy

    * How are you feeling?
        - I'm feeling happy.
        - I'm feeling furious.
    * How is he feeling?
        - He is feeling amused
    * How is she feeling?
        - She is feeling annoyed.
        - She is feeling cheerful.
        _ She is not feeling excited.

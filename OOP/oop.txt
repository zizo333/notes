* Class(entity): is a blueprint of an object. -> (attribuets/properties & behaviors/methods/actions )
* Object: is an instance of the class.
* Constructor: is a special type of method. (used for initialization || use the name of class)
* Access Modifiers:
  1. public (all)
  2. protected (class , package , subclass)
  3. no modifier (class , package)
  4. private (class)
